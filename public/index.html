About


I'm on my 3rd year of Sotftware Engineering at the University of Victoria. I am originally from Guatemala. I'm an extroverted and friendly guy.

I started programming at the age of 13. I started learning DarkBasic, a Basic-based language used for video game development. I got even more engaged with programming when I was 18. I started taking an algorithms course at a university in Guatemala. I immediately recognized my love for programming.

Lately, I have mostly been focusing on web development. I have worked with AngularJS on school projects, and with Ruby on Rails and Rendr on co-ops. I am also quite interested in React.js. My favorite language is C++. I love having more direct control over the memory while still having a fast language with the advantages of OOP. I am also a huge fan of smart pointers.

My hobbies are playing tennis, scuba diving, reading about philosophy and quantum physics, programming, and playing video games.
